derpol_legendre

PURPOSE

DERPOL_LEGENDRE recursive construction of Legendre polynomials 1st derivative

function [dlnp1]=derpol_legendre(n,ln,dln,dlnm1)

 DERPOL_LEGENDRE  recursive construction of Legendre polynomials 1st derivative
            formula (2.3.19), pag. 77, CHQZ2

 Input: n = polynomial degree
